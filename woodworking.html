CLINTON BUEHRING

In my spare time I love making stuff out of wood. It takes me back to Jr High Shop class.

It started with a Wine Rack that I made for my fiance. From there I've made a coffee table, dinning room table(in progress) and a wine rack for my mom. I've also created overhead pot and pan rack holders for the old house we rented out. Now that I'm free from grad school, I hope to work on more projects with my added free time.
