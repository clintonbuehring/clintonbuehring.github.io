CLINTON BUEHRING

Karnes City, the toughest small town in the Universe!

I grew up in Karnes City, Karnes for sort. The best way I can describe it's location to people I meet is it's the small town that you have to make a turn on when you travel to Port A. It's more than that though. It's a town of tough people and has been given the moniker as the toughest town in Texas by my friends. It's a town that slowly moves on into the future while still keeping a foot planted in the past...
